Stock Market Guide for Beginners in India (2026)

Aaj ke digital time me stock market ek powerful way ban chuka hai paisa grow karne ka. Lekin beginners ke liye sabse bada confusion hota hai – stock market kaise start kare? Agar aap bilkul new hain, to yeh complete stock market guide for beginners aapko step-by-step samjhayega.

Stock Market kya hota hai?

Stock market ek aisa place hai jahan companies apne shares sell karti hain aur log unhe buy karte hain. Jab aap kisi company ka share lete ho, to aap us company ke chhote se owner ban jaate ho.

Example: Agar aapne Reliance ka share liya, to aap us company me investment kar rahe ho.

Stock Market kaise kaam karta hai?

Market demand aur supply par chalta hai. Agar kisi stock ki demand zyada hai → price badhega Agar supply zyada hai → price girega

Isi movement par traders profit kamate hain.

Types of Trading

  • Intraday Trading: Same day buy-sell
  • Swing Trading: Few days hold
  • Long Term Investing: Months/years hold

Beginners kaise start kare?

Beginners ke liye best approach hai:

  • Basics samjho
  • Charts dekhna seekho
  • Practice karo bina risk ke

Direct real money se start karna risky hota hai, isliye aapko pehle ek stock market learning app ka use karna chahiye.

  • Without knowledge trading start karna
