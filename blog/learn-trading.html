How to Learn Trading Step by Step

Agar aap Google par search kar rahe ho "how to learn trading", to aap sahi jagah par ho. Trading ek skill hai jo time aur practice se develop hoti hai.

Step 1: Basics clear karo

Sabse pehle aapko yeh samajhna hoga:

  • Stock market kya hai
  • Shares kaise kaam karte hain
  • Market kaise move karta hai

Step 2: Charts samjho

Trading ka core hai charts. Aapko candlestick patterns samajhne honge:

  • Bullish candle
  • Bearish candle
  • Doji

Step 3: Indicators use karo

Indicators aapko decision lene me help karte hain:

  • RSI
  • MACD
  • Moving Average

Step 4: Practice (Most Important)

Beginners ki sabse badi galti hoti hai bina practice ke trading start karna. Aapko pehle trading simulation app ka use karna chahiye.

BullBear Market app aapko yeh facility deta hai jahan aap:

  • Virtual trading kar sakte ho
  • Portfolio track kar sakte ho
  • Leaderboard me compete kar sakte ho

Step 5: Risk Management

Har trade me risk hota hai. Isliye:

  • Stop loss lagao
  • Overtrading mat karo
  • Capital ko divide karo

Step 6: Strategy banao

Successful traders hamesha ek strategy follow karte hain. Random trading se loss hota hai.

Conclusion

Trading seekhna mushkil nahi hai agar aap sahi approach follow karo. Consistency aur discipline sabse important hai.
