Ultimate Trading Guide – Part 2 (Advanced Level)

This is the advanced section of trading where you move from beginner to professional level. If you master the concepts below, you can significantly improve your trading performance.

English Version

1. Price Action Trading (Core of Trading)

Price action trading is the foundation of professional trading. It focuses on understanding how price moves rather than depending only on indicators.

Traders study: - Market structure - Support & Resistance - Breakouts - Trend continuation

Price action gives you the pure psychology of market participants.

2. Market Structure

Market moves in patterns:

  • Higher High (HH)
  • Higher Low (HL)
  • Lower High (LH)
  • Lower Low (LL)

Understanding this helps you identify trend direction.

3. Support and Resistance

Support = price stops falling Resistance = price stops rising

These are key zones where traders take decisions.

4. Breakout Trading

Breakout happens when price crosses a strong level.

Rules: - Wait for confirmation - Avoid fake breakouts - Enter after retest

5. Indicators Advanced Use

Indicators are tools, not magic.

  • RSI → Momentum
  • MACD → Trend strength
  • Moving Average → Direction

Never rely on single indicator.

6. Risk Management (Most Important)

Without risk management, no trader survives.

  • Risk only 1–2% per trade
  • Always use stop loss
  • Follow risk-reward ratio

7. Risk Reward Ratio

Example: Risk = ₹100 Target = ₹200

This gives long-term profitability.

8. Trading Psychology

90% trading is psychology.

  • Control emotions
  • Avoid revenge trading
  • Stay disciplined

9. Common Mistakes

  • Overtrading
  • No strategy
  • Following tips blindly
  • Greed & fear

10. Daily Routine

Professional traders follow a system:

  • Market analysis
  • Trade planning
  • Execution
  • Review

11. Advanced Strategies

- Trend Following Strategy - Breakout Strategy - Scalping Strategy - Swing Trading

12. Final Advice

Trading is not a shortcut to money. It is a skill that takes time, patience, and discipline.
