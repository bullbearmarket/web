Risk Management Guide – Part 2 (Advanced Level)

ENGLISH VERSION (ADVANCED)

1. Drawdown Management

Drawdown means the reduction in your trading capital from peak to lowest point.

Example: You start with ₹10,000 Grow to ₹15,000 Then fall to ₹12,000

Drawdown = ₹3,000 (20%)

Professional traders always control drawdown below 10–15%.

2. Maximum Loss Limit (Daily Rule)

Always define daily loss limit.

Example: Capital = ₹10,000 Daily loss limit = 2% = ₹200

If you lose ₹200 → STOP trading for the day.

This protects you from emotional damage.

3. Risk Per Trade vs Total Risk

Per trade risk is different from total exposure.

Example: Trade 1 → ₹100 risk Trade 2 → ₹100 risk Trade 3 → ₹100 risk

Total exposure = ₹300

So always control total risk.

4. Compounding Strategy

Risk management helps you grow capital slowly.

Example: Day 1 → ₹10,000 Day 30 → ₹12,000 Day 60 → ₹15,000

Small consistent gains create big wealth.

5. Risk Adjustment Based on Market

Market conditions change:

  • Trending → Increase size
  • Sideways → Reduce risk
  • Volatile → Trade carefully

Professional traders adjust risk dynamically.

6. Multiple Stop Loss Techniques

Types of stop loss:

  • Fixed Stop Loss
  • Technical Stop Loss
  • Trailing Stop Loss

Example: Buy at ₹100 Price goes to ₹110 Move stop loss from ₹95 → ₹105

Now trade is risk-free.

7. Capital Protection Strategy

Rule:

  • First save capital
  • Then grow capital

Never try to double money quickly.

8. Professional Trader Mindset

Pros think like this:

  • Loss is part of trading
  • Focus on process, not outcome
  • Consistency over jackpot

9. Real Example (Powerful)

Trader A: Risk = 10% per trade 5 losses → Account almost gone

Trader B: Risk = 1% 5 losses → Still strong

This is why risk management decides success.

10. Advanced Golden Rules

  • Control drawdown
  • Limit daily loss
  • Adjust risk with market
  • Use trailing stop loss
  • Think long term

In trading, survival = success.

हिंदी संस्करण (एडवांस)

1. ड्रॉडाउन क्या होता है?

ड्रॉडाउन का मतलब है आपके अकाउंट में गिरावट।

Example: ₹10,000 → ₹15,000 → ₹12,000 Loss = ₹3,000

इसे कंट्रोल करना बहुत जरूरी है।

2. डेली लॉस लिमिट

हर दिन का loss fix करो।

₹10,000 capital ₹200 loss → stop trading

3. कुल रिस्क (Total Risk)

एक साथ ज्यादा ट्रेड मत खोलो।

3 ट्रेड = 3x risk

4. कंपाउंडिंग

छोटा profit धीरे-धीरे बड़ा बनता है।

5. मार्केट के हिसाब से रिस्क

Trending → ज्यादा Sideways → कम Volatile → सावधानी

6. ट्रेलिंग स्टॉप लॉस

7. कैपिटल प्रोटेक्शन

पहले पैसा बचाओ फिर बढ़ाओ।

8. प्रो ट्रेडर माइंडसेट

  • Loss normal है

  • Consistency ही जीत है

9. रियल उदाहरण
